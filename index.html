About me

I am a tenured assistant professor in the School of Data Science and Engineering at Guangdong Polytechnic Normal University (GPNU). I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Department of Computing from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2024, with most of my research done at COMPASS Lab of SUSTech, co-advised by Prof. Fengwei Zhang and Prof. Xiapu Luo. Before that, I received my M.S. and B.S. degrees in Department of Computer Science and Engineering from South China University of Technology and University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2020 and 2017, respectively.

Research

My primary research interest is system security, including confidential computing, trusted execution environment, and hardware-assisted security with papers published in top-tier venues (e.g., USENIX Security, NDSS, CCS).
